About

Who You Are

I am a PhD student in computational chemistry at Rutgers-Newark University. I am interested in the development of new methods for computational chemistry and the application of these methods to the study of chemical reactions and materials. I am currently working in Orbital-Free Density Functional Theory (OF-DFT) and its time-dependent extension (OF-TDDFT). I am also interested on the develoment of machine learning methods for quantum chemestry. I develop software and workflows using python packages like DFTpy, QEpy, pyscf, scickitlearn, etc.
