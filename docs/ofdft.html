Orbital‑Free Density Functional Theory

To understand Orbital-Free Density Functional Theory (OF-DFT) we need to understand the basics of Density Functional Theory (DFT). The breakthrough of DFT was the realization that the ground state properties of a system can be fully described by the electron density n(r) and more importantly the ground state electron density is uniquely determined by the external potential V_{ext}(\vec{r}) (Hahenberg-Kohn theorem).
